Dashboard Overview

Gauge Display

The gauge shows current PM2.5 levels with color indicators:

  • Green (0-12 μg/m³): Good air quality
  • Yellow (12-35 μg/m³): Moderate levels
  • Red (>35 μg/m³): Poor air quality
